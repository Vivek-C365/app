# Product Overview

## Animal Rescue Platform

A web application designed to connect people in India who discover injured or distressed animals with local volunteers and NGOs who can provide immediate assistance.

### Core Purpose
- **Emergency Response**: Enable quick reporting of animal emergencies with photos and location
- **Community Connection**: Match reporters with nearby verified volunteers and NGOs using geospatial queries
- **Real-time Coordination**: Facilitate communication and status tracking throughout rescue operations

### Key Features
- Quick animal reporting with photo and location capture
- Location-based matching of volunteers and NGOs
- Multi-channel notifications (WhatsApp, email, push notifications)
- Real-time messaging for case coordination
- Mobile-first PWA design for accessibility
- User verification system for volunteers and NGOs
- AI emergency assistance when no volunteers respond
- Mandatory 24-hour status updates with photo requirements
- Case timeline and status tracking

### Target Users
- **Reporters**: General public discovering animals in distress
- **Volunteers**: Individual animal welfare advocates
- **NGOs**: Registered animal welfare organizations
- **Admins**: Platform administrators managing verification and moderation

### Business Logic
- Cases require immediate attention with escalation mechanisms
- Status updates are mandatory every 24 hours with photo proof
- AI assistance activates when no volunteers respond within timeframes
- Geospatial matching prioritizes proximity for faster response
- Verification system ensures helper credibility
